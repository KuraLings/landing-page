Meet Our Pets

Aphrodite/Psyche

The ruler of relaxation and empress of extra treats. This senior gal was surrendered to our rescue through no fault of her own. Understandably, she is a bit of unsure of the world right now and is not her usual cuddly self. We believe with time and the right adopter, she will get back to her old ways of enjoying the company of humans. Her plumper physique necessitates she continue on her weight loss journey and would love a home where her humans can continue to support a healthy and active lifestyle.

Mipha

An unaltered female black and white approximately 5 month old Aussie Heeler mix. She is very sweet, loves attention, shy and little timid. Mipha is looking for a family that will help her come out of her shell and build her confidence up. She would do best with other dogs, great with little kids and other animals.

Yuzu

Typical kitten that likes to run, play, and explore. She is a talker that will seek out attention. She is the first to explore new things. To Avoid Single Kitten Syndrome, she must be adopted with one of her siblings or into a home that has a social cat/kitten to be his companion and playmate.
